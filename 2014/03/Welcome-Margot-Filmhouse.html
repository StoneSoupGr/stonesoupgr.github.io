We welcome Margot Filmhouse at Stone Soup

Published01 Mar 2014

Margot Filmhouse's relocated its base at Stone Soup, just a block away from their old space, still and always in central Athens. Margot Filmhouse specializes in film production, digital narrative, and digital advertising. Visit their website www.margotfilmhouse.com for news about their cool projects!

Together with Margot Filmhouse we have built a post-production room to be used by Stone Soup members and affiliates. Come check it out!
